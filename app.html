Help

Double click on a cell to edit in or hover over it and click on the edit icon. After editing a cell, hit the enter key to save it (You can also click on the check icon alternatively) Click on a the check icon to save the task. To add an item to your project, click the "add item" button at the top right corner. You can choose a custom template project, by clicking on the template button at the top right corner, or create a custom template by editing the table rows yourself. To delete an item, click on the trash icon of the row you want to remove. You can add team members to your project by selecting the "team members" button udner the project title. You can also add members to individual tasks as well by hvering over a task udner the "Team Member" column. Automate your tasks and projects by creating custom automations by clicking on the "Create automation" button under the project title. Swap item positions in table by selecting the "Reorder Items" button under the project title. Edit project name and color by selecting the "edit project" button under the project title.
